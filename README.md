# Sales Insights Dashboard - AtliQ Hardware
### About the company 
#### AtliQ Hardware is a computer hardware and peripheral manufacturing company operating across multiple regions in India. It supplies products to various retail stores and is headquartered in Delhi.
### Problem Statement
#### AtliQ Hardware is experiencing rapid growth in a highly dynamic market. However, the organization faces significant challenges in effectively tracking and analyzing sales performance across different regions.
#### Currently, sales data is distributed across multiple Excel files maintained by various regional teams. This leads to a lack of data centralization and standardization. As a result, whenever management requests insights—such as quarterly growth or regional performance—teams provide raw Excel files instead of structured, actionable insights.
#### This fragmented approach makes data analysis time-consuming, difficult to interpret, and inefficient for decision-making. Additionally, the absence of real-time reporting and visual dashboards limits the organization’s ability to monitor performance and respond quickly to business changes.
